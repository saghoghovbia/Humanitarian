How it works


After signing up, users are able to connect with groups and introduce themselves to others around the world.

When finding groups, users are able to filter based on age range, interest, and location and, even level/experience depending on the interest. We also have an active forum public to all users despite the group association to enable members to connect, learn, and share freely with one another.

So-Cial has partnered with Talkspace to monitor the progress of those afflicted from social withdrawals joining our community. For those recommended by a doctor, a schedule will be created working with them. For those a part of the community, there are regularly discounted sessions all year round!
